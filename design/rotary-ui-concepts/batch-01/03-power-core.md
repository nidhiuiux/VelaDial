# Concept 03: Large Center Power Button (Power-Core)

This UI prioritizes the most common action: turning the lights on and off. The entire center of the screen acts as a massive, unmissable touch target.

**Key Features:**
- Giant circular power button filling 80% of the screen
- Large power icon
- Clear "ON" or "OFF" status text below
- Room name at the top

**Why it works for VelaDial:**
It is impossible to miss the touch target, even in the dark or when groggy. It treats the rotary display primarily as a smart light switch, with brightness and presets relegated to secondary pages.
