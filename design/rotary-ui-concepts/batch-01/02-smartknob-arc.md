# Concept 02: SmartKnob-Inspired Arc

This concept emphasizes the physical relationship between the rotary knob and the visual interface. A thick, bold amber arc hugs the outer edge of the display, filling up as the knob is turned clockwise.

**Key Features:**
- Thick, prominent edge arc
- Center percentage text
- Small "BRIGHTNESS" label
- A small dot indicator at the leading edge of the arc

**Why it works for VelaDial:**
It provides excellent tactile-visual feedback. The thick arc makes it easy to see the approximate brightness level out of the corner of your eye without needing to read the exact number.
