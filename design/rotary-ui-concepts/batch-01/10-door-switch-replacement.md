# Concept 10: Door Switch Replacement

This concept visually mimics a physical Decora-style rocker switch, splitting the round screen horizontally.

**Key Features:**
- Top half acts as the "ON" button
- Bottom half acts as the "OFF" button
- Active state is highlighted with an amber outline and fill
- Small brightness indicator at the very bottom

**Why it works for VelaDial:**
It is instantly understandable to guests who have never used a smart home controller before. However, it doesn't utilize the round shape of the display very elegantly.
