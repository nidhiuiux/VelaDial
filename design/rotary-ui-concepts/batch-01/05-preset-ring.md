# Concept 05: Preset Ring UI

Focused on mood lighting, this concept arranges color and temperature presets in a circle around the perimeter of the display, leaving the center for status information.

**Key Features:**
- 6 colored preset dots around the edge
- Center brightness percentage
- Active preset name displayed below the percentage
- The active preset dot is slightly larger and filled

**Why it works for VelaDial:**
It provides fast, one-tap access to different lighting moods without needing to swipe to a dedicated preset page. However, the touch targets are small, which may be challenging on a 1.28" screen.
